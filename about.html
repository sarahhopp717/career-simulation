ABOUT ME

When I'm not working my booty off, I enjoy the following things

YOGA

Yoga has literally changed my life. I got into a pretty bad accident in college. I got hit by a bus, yikes! I wasnt able to even bend down and touch my knees. Now, after over 10 years of consistently going since that accident, I can comfortably say that I am a real Yogi, and I wouldn't change it for anything.

DRUMMER

I started playing the drums when I was 20. I got an acoustic kit from a buddy in high school that was getting deployed and had no use for his kit anymore. What an aventure it has been to go from never thinking I was "cool" enough to be a drummer, to then having multiple YouTube channels where I have solely created my own tracks for people to view and listen. Finding an instrument that can match my ADHD brain has been so beneficial for me. Having six different things to do at once was easier for me than just playing the piano!

CAMPING

After getting married in 2022, my wife and I sold all of our belongings, besides the essentials, and bought a trailer to travel the country for a whole year. It was the best experience, I would recommend to anyone that wants to really connect with nature, and test their limits with budgeting, planning, and everything that comes with traveling.
